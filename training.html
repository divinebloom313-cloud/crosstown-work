Your Rights as a W-2 Worker

Massachusetts Temporary Workers Right to Know, your pay rights, and how to report workplace issues.

You are a W-2 employee of Crosstown Work — not a contractor. You are entitled to minimum wage ($15/hr minimum in Massachusetts), workers compensation, and a safe workplace.
